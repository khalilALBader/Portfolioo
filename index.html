Latest Project

Medical project

Ri’ayah is a free, offline, and user-friendly medical health care application designed to diagnose illnesses without the need for a doctor. It can diagnose various health conditions and identify skin diseases by analyzing a picture of the affected area. Additionally, Ri’ayah offers detailed descriptions and result ranges for common blood tests, helping patients understand their health without needing to visit a doctor. Ri’ayah simplifies medical diagnostics, making healthcare accessible and convenient.
